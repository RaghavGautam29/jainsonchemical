About Us

History

Jainson Lab (INDIA) is India's leading private sector manufacturer, exporter and supplier of Sulphur and Pyrites. All products are tandem with industry laid standards and so for the reason highly appreciated by the clients across the region. Available in various specifications, these products are acknowledged for their excellent performance. Jainson Lab (INDIA) is our manufacturing unit established in 1990. , a young entrepreneur from the fabled town , realised early in life that business was all about spotting opportunities, and seizing them wherever they surfaced. He travelled to Meerut in 1975 , and quickly learnt the ropes of business. The business journey, which began in Meerut, entered the international arena with an office in Mumbai and export all around the globe.

Our mission

Our Mission is to increase the knowledge acquired in over half a century of processing mineral products and continuously develop product performance, in various fields of application, ensuring customer delight and optimizing utilization of resources by adopting most modern technological advancements to manufacture high quality products.

Our Vision

Jainson Lab India would be a global leader, known for its superior quality products and customer oriented solutions. Besides being a benchmark in the mineral industry we serve we would uphold the notion of being a responsible corporate citizen by whole heartedly contributing to the society and the world at large.

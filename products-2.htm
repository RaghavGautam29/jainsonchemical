Pyrite

Pyrite is brass yellow in color. It has a bright metallic lustrous looking stone. Pyrite is very well known stone & most common sulfide mineral. It's chemical composition of iron disulfide is FeS2. Pyrite is considered to be an essential ore in production of sulfur & sulfuric acid. Pyrite is also brittle in its structural form & will break easily. Pyrite has a famous nickname -"Fool's Gold". Pyrite & gold can be easily differentiated. Pyrite is also very well-known for its meta-physical and healing properties. It occurs in sedimentary rocks as framboidal crystal.
